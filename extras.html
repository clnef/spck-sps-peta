1. The Balete

One of the best places to chill, have conversations, and plan for PETAs.

The oldest tree that SFHS takes care of, protected with fences and made to be a place to hang out with classmates with it's circular bench, the cement log-like chairs which you can use to sit and a table.

Located near the covered court, Admin Building and Pimentel Building, this spot is often times free during recess and end of classes.
